##  What is Digital Literacy

Digital literacy refers to the ability to use digital tools and technologies effectively, safely, and responsibly. It includes skills such as using the internet, communicating online, and protecting personal information.

---

##  Useful Digital Tools

- Canva – For creating designs and infographics  
- GitHub – For managing projects  
- LinkedIn – For professional networking  
- Google Forms – For surveys and quizzes  

---

##  Safe Internet Practices

- Use strong passwords  
- Avoid clicking unknown links  
- Keep personal information private  
- Use secure websites  

---

##  Professional Online Behaviour

- Be respectful in communication  
- Maintain a professional profile  
- Avoid inappropriate content  
- Communicate clearly  

---

##  Email Etiquette

- Use proper subject lines  
- Maintain formal tone  
- Avoid slang  
- Be clear and concise  

---

##  Benefits of Digital Literacy

- Better communication skills  
- Improved job opportunities  
- Increased online safety  
- Better access to information
